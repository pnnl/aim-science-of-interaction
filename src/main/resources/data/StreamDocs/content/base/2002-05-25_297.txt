Title: ELODIS PUBLIC HEALTH FACT SHEET 2002
Source: Homeland Illumination
Author: Maha Salo
Date: 2002-05-25
<p>ELODIS, Kronos -The Elodis Community Health Center has published an update to the Wellness for All study performed in 1998.  The effects of Hyper Acidic Substrate Removal (HASR), a controversial drilling technique continue to ravage the health of Elodis residents.  The full report will soon be posted on the Elodis municipal website.</p>
<p>Highlights</p>
<p>* The mortality rate and incidence of chronic illness increased  over 178% from prior years</p>
<p>* The unusually high incidence of cancer became so apparent that Minister of Health Nespola appointed an oncology specialist to Abila General Hospital</p>
