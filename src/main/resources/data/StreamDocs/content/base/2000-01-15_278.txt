Title: KRONOS GDP ON THE RISE
Source: The World
Author: 
Date: 2000-01-15
<p>ABILA, Kronos - The real gross domestic product - the output of goods and services produced by labor and property located in Kronos - increased at an annual rate of 5.4% in the fourth quarter of 1999, according to the advance estimate released by the Bureau of Economics.</p>
<p>This marks the 5th year in a row with positive GDP growth for the developing country. Most of this growth can be contributed to the relationship between the Government of Kronos and GAStech International.</p>
