Title: POK Kidnaps GAStech Employees 
Source: News Online Today
Author: 
Date: 2014-01-20
<p>2014/01/20</p>
<p>ABILA, Kronos - The Protectors of Kronos (POK) are suspected of kidnapping up to fourteen GAStech employees from their headquarters building earlier today.  One employee, Edvard Vann of Abila, was questioned for hours because he shares a family name with several known POK members.</p>
