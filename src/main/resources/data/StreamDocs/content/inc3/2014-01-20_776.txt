Title: Breaking: Emergency at GAStech Headquarters Building [Updates]
Source: Kronos Star
Author: Cato Rossini, Marcella Trapani
Date: 2014-01-20
<p>Update, 7:35 PM:  Abila police spokesman Adrien Carman said, "Several GasTech employees appear to be missing. Anyone seeing unusual activity is asked to contact the Abila police."  In response to question about whether a kidnapping is suspected, Carman replied, "The case is under investigation and more information will be released as facts are learned. Please report any unusual activity to the Abila police."</p>
