Title: KIDNAPPING AT GASTECH KRONOS  
Source: News Online Today
Author: 
Date: 2014-01-20
<p>2014/01/20</p>
<p>ABILA, Kronos - After a long day of conflicting and confusing information reports are still sketchy, but the story is beginning to come clear: a Kronos Government source has confirmed up to fourteen of GAStech International's employees may have been kidnapped at the company's headquarters in Abila. Abila Fire Department trucks were dispatched to GAStech - Kronos headquarters around 10:00 this morning local Kronos time to respond to a fire alarm.</p>
