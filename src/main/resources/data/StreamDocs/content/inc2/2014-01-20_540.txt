Title: Breaking: Emergency at GAStech Headquarters Building [Updates]
Source: Kronos Star
Author: Cato Rossini, Marcella Trapani
Date: 2014-01-20
<p>Update, 11:55 PM:  Abila Fire Captain Federigo Accardi announces that it was a false alarm and the building is safe.  In response to reporter's questions, Captain Accardi says GAStech employees made no report of a bomb threat to his agency.</p>
