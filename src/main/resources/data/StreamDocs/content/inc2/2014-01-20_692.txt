Title: Breaking: Emergency at GAStech Headquarters Building [Updates]
Source: Kronos Star
Author: Cato Rossini, Marcella Trapani
Date: 2014-01-20
<p>At approximately 10:15 AM this morning, the fire alarm went off at the GAStech headquarters building in downtown Abila.  At the time of the alarm, the media had gathered in front of the building to cover the meeting of GAStech executives, including GAStech President and CEO Sten Sanjorge Jr. and local GAStech executives and their subsequent transition to the presidentially hosted reception at the Capitol Building.</p>
