Title: GAStech's Sanjorge Escapes Kidnapping at GAStech HQ 
Source: News Online Today
Author: 
Date: 2014-01-21
<p>2014/01/21</p>
<p>ABILA, Kronos - According to a press conference held at GAStech headquarters in Centrum, Tethys this morning, GAStech's President and CEO, Sten Sanjorge Jr.  Sanjorge, who had been attending a corporate meeting, was in transit from the GAStech building to the capitol when the kidnapping took place.</p>
